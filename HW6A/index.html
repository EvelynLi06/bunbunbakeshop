About us

Hi, friends! Welcome to Bun Bun Bake Shop. We are a small bakery that specializes in cinnamon rolls, from our famous original cinnamon rolls to a variety of artisan flavors. Our shop is located in Pittsburgh, PA and we ship fresh rolls anywhere in the Pittsburgh area.
